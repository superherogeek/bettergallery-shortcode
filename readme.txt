A Responsive Gallery shortcode with the ability to Paginate your gallery and more!

== Description ==

***Features and Usage***
Better Gallery has the features of the standard [gallery] shortcode PLUS...

***Paginate your gallery***
Choose how many images you would like to display per page and Better Gallery handles the rest.
  [bettergallery images_per_page="number of images"]

***Responsive for ALL Devices***
Better Gallery is responsive gallery for WordPress and adapts itself to all devices. Your gallery will be ‘swipeable’ on mobile phones. No extra effort is needed! Just resize your browser window to 480px wide or below to see it in action!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `osp-bettergallery` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place use `[bettergallery]` shortcode in place of '[gallery]' to display images in your pages and posts
1. Go to 'Settings' > 'Better Gallery' for additional configuration
